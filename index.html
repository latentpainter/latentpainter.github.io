Turning diffuser prediction into painting actions

How

Abstract

The latent diffuser models gain a lot of traction in generative AI for their efficiency, content diversity, and reasonable footprint.

Background

The latent diffuser predicts better and better final outputs along denoising process, which can be used as animation frames. However, the majority of the update is completed during the first few frames -- making the rest of the animation stall (Fig. 1). In a different scenario, animation transits between two generated images via interpolating both the embedded prompts and seeded latents (Fig. 2). While the between-image animation provides high quality morphology, it also comes with occasional disruptions and unrealistic scenes.
